THE BEST MOVIE ACCORDING TO HAARIS AHMED

TOP 3 MOVIES ACCORDING TO ME

MS DHONI THE UNTOLD STORY

MS Dhoni: The Untold Story is a 2016 Indian biographical sports drama film directed by Neeraj Pandey. The movie stars Sushant Singh Rajput in the lead role, portraying the journey of legendary Indian cricketer Mahendra Singh Dhoni. It also features Kiara Advani, Disha Patani, and Anupam Kher in pivotal roles. The film chronicles Dhoni’s life from his childhood in Ranchi to his rise as one of India’s most successful cricket captains. It explores his early struggles, his passion for the sport, and his determination to overcome obstacles. Despite societal pressure to pursue a government job, Dhoni follows his dream, eventually earning a place in the Indian cricket team. The movie highlights key moments of his career, including India’s historic 2011 World Cup victory, where Dhoni’s iconic six sealed the win.

With Sushant Singh Rajput’s outstanding performance, gripping storytelling, and Amaal Mallik’s soulful music, MS Dhoni: The Untold Story is an inspiring tribute to the cricketing legend. The film resonates with audiences, showcasing the power of perseverance, dedication, and self-belief. It remains one of the most loved sports biopics in Indian cinema.

MY NAME IS KHAN

My Name Is Khan is a 2010 Indian drama film directed by Karan Johar, starring Shah Rukh Khan and Kajol. The film is a powerful story about love, identity, and the challenges faced by Muslims in a post-9/11 world. Shah Rukh Khan plays Rizwan Khan, a man with Asperger’s syndrome who moves to the United States. He falls in love with Mandira (Kajol), a Hindu single mother, and they get married. However, after the tragic events of 9/11, their lives take a heartbreaking turn. Mandira's son dies in a hate crime, and she blames Rizwan, leading him to embark on a journey to prove his innocence and humanity. His mission is simple yet profound: to meet the U.S. President and say, "My name is Khan, and I am not a terrorist." The film beautifully portrays themes of love, discrimination, and perseverance. Shah Rukh Khan delivers one of his finest performances, earning widespread praise. With soulful music by Shankar-Ehsaan-Loy and a deeply emotional narrative, My Name Is Khan is a thought-provoking film that leaves a lasting impact.

BILLU BARBER

Billu (also known as Billu Barber) is a 2009 Indian Hindi-language drama film directed by Priyadarshan. The film stars Irrfan Khan in the titular role, alongside Lara Dutta, while Shah Rukh Khan appears in an extended cameo. It is an adaptation of the Malayalam film Kadha Parayumbol (2007). The story revolves around Billu, a humble barber in a small village, who struggles to make ends meet with his wife (Lara Dutta) and two children. His life takes an unexpected turn when Bollywood superstar Sahir Khan (Shah Rukh Khan) arrives in the village for a film shoot. Rumors spread that Billu was once Sahir’s childhood friend, leading the villagers to shower him with sudden admiration. However, Billu, hesitant and insecure about his social status, avoids meeting Sahir, fearing rejection. The climax delivers an emotional twist when Sahir publicly acknowledges their friendship, highlighting the value of true relationships beyond fame and fortune. Billu beautifully portrays themes of friendship, humility, and societal pressures. With heartfelt performances, soulful music by Pritam, and emotional depth, the film is a touching tale about self-worth and enduring bonds. Irrfan Khan’s performance stands out, making Billu a memorable cinematic experience.
